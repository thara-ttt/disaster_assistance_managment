Disaster Assistance Management System

Disaster management system where recipients can request resourses related to disaster events and Donors can assist recipients.

Features

Our Disaster Assistance Management System provides a wide range of features to Donors and Recipeints.

Ask for Assistance

Recipeint users can choose disaster events and the required resource

Donate resourses

Donors can navigate through recipient requests and give required resources

Pledge

Donors can pledge resources which will be matched to deserving recipient
